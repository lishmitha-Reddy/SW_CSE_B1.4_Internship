Ordered list

Ordered lists are used to display items in a specific order, usually with numbers or letters. They are useful for showing steps in a process or ranking items.

  • First item
  • Second item
  • Third item

Ordered lists can also be nested within each other to create sub-lists. This is useful for showing hierarchical relationships between items.

  • First item
    • Sub-item 1
    • Sub-item 2
  • Second item
  • Third item

Ordered lists can also be customized with different numbering styles, such as Roman numer als or letters. This can be done using the type attribute in the ol tag.

  1. First item
  2. Second item
  3. Third item

Overall, ordered lists are a useful tool for organizing information in a clear and structured way. They can be used in a variety of contexts, from simple lists to complex hierarchies, and can be customized to fit the needs of the content being presented.
